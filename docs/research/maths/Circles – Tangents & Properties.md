### 🧠 How the Brain Chunks This Chapter

The brain encodes this as **rule-based geometry system**:

1. **Line-Circle Interaction Model**

   * No intersection → no tangent
   * 1 intersection → tangent
   * 2 intersections → secant

2. **Core Theorem Anchor**

   * Tangent ⟂ Radius

3. **Symmetry Rule**

   * Tangents from external point are equal

4. **Case-Based Logic**

   * Inside → 0 tangents
   * On → 1 tangent
   * Outside → 2 tangents
