## 🎯 WHAT TO DO — Simple Daily Flow

### 1️⃣ Morning (First 20 min after waking)

```
☐ Stay in bed, eyes closed
☐ Ask: "What did I learn yesterday?"
☐ Try to recall WITHOUT looking
☐ Notice gaps → today's priority
```

_Template: Morning Retrieval Log_

---

### 2️⃣ Main Session (Your study time)

```
☐ 2 min breath + intention
☐ Say: "I am a problem solver"
☐ 25 min INPUT (learn new thing)
☐ 10 min REFLECT (why does it work?)
☐ 30 min PRACTICE (struggle without hints)
☐ 5 min COMPRESS (one-sentence rule)
```

_Template: Daily Learning Log_

---

### 3️⃣ Evening (Before entertainment)

```
☐ 10 min light review
☐ Explain what you learned (to yourself)
☐ Update your Playbook
☐ Journal: what worked/didn't
```

_Template: Daily Learning Log_

---

### 4️⃣ Pre-Sleep (Last 10 min)

```
☐ Lie down, lights dim
☐ Replay today's pattern in mind
☐ Visualize solving it easily
☐ Say: "This is part of me now"
☐ Fall asleep with pattern in mind
```

_Template: Pre-Sleep Seeding Log_
